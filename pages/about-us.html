About Us

Planted not Purchased

We do not take shortcuts. We care for your flowers from the time they are planted to the time they are delivered. So when you call us, you can be assured that we know our products inside and out. Your flowers are grown in specialized greenhouses so that their leaves are clean, stems are straight, colors are crisp, and will have the maximum vase life.

We understand that our flowers are used to deliver your special personal messages, so we focus as much on your flowers as the wrappers they come in. It ensures the maximum impact when they are delivered and displayed.
