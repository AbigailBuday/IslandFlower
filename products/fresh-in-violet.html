Shipping Details

Island Rose can deliver your flowers any day of the week. Same-day delivery is available in Metro Manila from Monday to Saturday for orders placed before 3:30PM. Sunday delivery is available for Metro Manila and some provinces for orders placed before 4:00PM on Saturday.

Due to COVID-19, our provincial deliveries are limited to a few nearby provinces. Updates on availability are announced on our homepage and social media. Please do not hesitate to contact us for enquiries so we can see if we can find a way to assist you.

We deliver farm fresh flowers and premium gift items from 11:00AM to 5:00PM. This may sometimes extend to 7:00PM onwards depending on the number of deliveries for the day. We cannot accommodate time-specific deliveries as our courier uses the most optimal route to assure that all deliveries are fulfilled within the day.

We take every precaution to make sure that your flowers and gifts arrive at their destination on the date specified. However, there are some delivery areas that take 2-3 days transit time especially during peak seasons. To avoid delays, kindly place your order 2 - 3 days prior to your desired delivery date.

For assistance, email us at helpdesk@islandrose.net or call us at +632 8623845. Our office hours are from Mondays to Saturdays, 7:30AM to 5:00PM Philippine Time

Packaging

Every rose is hand-picked by our meticulous rose farmers and arranged by our dedicated staff. The flowers are preserved in water with their very own floral tube to make sure they get to you in perfect condition.

Island Rose bouquets are packaged in supple wrapping material with matching ribbons. Simply unwrap the bouquet, remove the floral tube, trim the stems, and put them in a vase of clean water. And voilà, instant happiness!Let the Island Rose greeting card deliver your message of love.

Our bespoke greeting cards can accommodate up to 50 words to express your deepest affections.

Our exclusive shipping box is specially designed to get your flowers safely to their destination. Flowers are delivered via first-class couriers.
